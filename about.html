Who am I?

Currently I am a graduate student in industrial automation at, HoGent, working on my Bachelor's thesis. My thesis is focused around improving the practicals and linking the different subject, throughout the education, with each other. You can read more about my thesis here.

I am a big fan and supporter of (open) education. As such all the personal projects I do and make, whether they will be a success or not, will be open-source and well documented. As long as we all keep an open mind and share what we know, we can all learn something from each other. You are never to old to learn!
In fact one of my goals and aspirations if life is to become a professor, so that I can teach others even more.

I have a big passion for (vintage) computers and electronics. I like to work with logic gates and digital circuits in my free time. I plan on making computer using discrete logic gates, so keep an eye out for that.
